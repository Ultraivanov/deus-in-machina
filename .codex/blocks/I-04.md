### I-04-T2 — Hero/Card/List heuristics + confidence

**Files to modify:**
- `src/patterns.js` — add Hero/Card/List heuristics and confidence scoring

**Files to create:**
- none

**Files NOT touched:**
- CLI, workflow engine, adapters, normalizer, validator, fixes, monetization, stripe, storage

**Approach:**
Add simple detectors over layout context to infer Hero (headline/subheadline/cta),
Card (container + title + description + optional media), and List (repeated item
structures). Each detector produces a deterministic confidence score based on
matched cues, and the final output filters by `minConfidence`.

**Risks:**
Heuristics rely on simplified layout naming and may need expansion for real trees.
